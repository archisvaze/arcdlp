ArcDLP
-
A simple desktop app for downloading videos and audio from YouTube, Vimeo, Twitter, Instagram, and thousands of other sites. Powered by yt-dlp.
How it works
Paste a video or playlist URL, pick your preferred quality, and download. Videos are saved as MP4, audio as MP3.
Where are my downloads?
By default, files are saved to your Downloads folder in a subfolder. You can change this in Settings. Use the "Open folder" button in the Queue tab to find your files quickly.
